How to build your files

  • Scale: ½ scale (build at half size, output at 200%)
  • Bleed: add 1.0″ on each side (2.0″ total to the overall width and height)
  • Color: CMYK / Pantone
  • Resolution: 120–150 ppi at scale (no more than 150)
  • Fonts: convert to outlines
  • Printer marks: disabled (no crop/registration/color bars)
  • Safe margin: keep logos & text ~4.0″ in from the edges
  • Submit via: WeTransfer, Dropbox, Adobe Creative Cloud
